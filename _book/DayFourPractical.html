The Reason Explained:

The reason for the size of this difference is because ExpressJet Airlines Inc. (carrier “EV”) only ever flew this route once and that flight happened to be severly delayed by 128 minutes. And because the airline only ever flew the route once its average arrival delay has not had the chance to ‘average out’ over multiple flights causing it be an outlier when compared to other routes whose average arrival delays conform far more to the law of averages.
